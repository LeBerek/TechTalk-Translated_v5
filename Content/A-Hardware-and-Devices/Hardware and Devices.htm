Hardware and Devices

Hardware and Devices are important in our digital world. They are real parts that make our computers work and help us use the digital world. We use a mouse to move on screens. Servers are strong and hold a lot of data. Terminals let us talk to other systems. All of these have an important job. We will look more at these three parts: the Mouse, the Server, and the Terminal.
