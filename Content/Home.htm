Frequently Asked Questions

Q.1 - Why?

Why is the subject of the project IT vocabulary? Because I am fascinated by the parallel world of simple words describing more or less complicated aspects of the digital world.

Q. 2 - What?

What determined the choice of words for this project? The plasticity of the terms. The fact that it was so easy to draw a tiny icon from the real world for each topic.

Q. 3 - When?

When will the project take full shape? The MVP stage will have to suffice as a full shape the project. This is my first project in Madcap and there are certainly many more options to try and apply better design principles, to use better tools and ideas. For the moment, the MVP has to suffice.
