Web Services

Web Services, a familiar term in the IT world, takes on a unique meaning distinct from its everyday usage. Just as a spider weaves intricate webs, “Spider” in IT refers to a program that crawls through the internet, gathering data. “Cookie”, more than a sweet treat, is a small piece of data stored on your computer by a web browser.
